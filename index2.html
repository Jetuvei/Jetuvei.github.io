About Me!

I am a PhD student, studying nonequilibrium stochastic processes, in the School of Physics and Astronomy at the University of Edinburgh. I am funded by the Scottish Universities Physics Alliance (SUPA) through the Condensed Matter Doctoral Training Centre (CM-DTC).

You can find me on Research Gate and LinkedIn.
